PriceWheel Project Details

Technologies Used:

  • Backend: Python, FastAPI
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
  • Machine Learning: Trained regression models for price prediction
  • Database: MySQL (for user authentication and listings)

🚗 Project Overview:

Price Wheel is a smart web-based platform that predicts the resale price of a car using AI/ML models and offers a user-friendly car marketplace for buying and selling vehicles.

🔍 Key Features:

  • Car Price Prediction: Users can estimate a car’s selling price by inputting details such as brand, model, year, fuel type, transmission, and more.
  • User Authentication:
    • Secure sign-up and login system using FastAPI and MySQL
    • Email verification for new account registration
    • Access control for logged-in users to use advanced features
  • Marketplace Functionality:
    • Users can list their cars for sale
    • Buyers can browse both user-listed and showroom-listed cars
    • Listings open in a new page (same tab) for smooth experience
  • Chat Box Feature (Inspired by Mia – NFS Most Wanted): A cool AI-style assistant chat box is planned for user interaction, support, and future smart search/filter features.

💡 Goal of the Project:

To simplify the car selling/buying process with AI-powered pricing, while building an all-in-one platform with secure login, car listing, and a modern UI/UX.

Medicure Project Details

Project Overview:

Medicure is an innovative health-tech project developed to simplify access to medicines and healthcare professionals. Designed during the COVID-19 pandemic, it aims to connect patients with nearby medical stores and doctors through a single platform. Users can search, upload prescriptions, purchase medicines, and even consult doctors (like dermatologists, endocrinologists, and family physicians) via video calls. Unlike existing platforms like Apollo or PharmEasy, Medicure acts as a mediator—enabling local medical stores and doctors to register and reach patients directly. This not only enhances accessibility but also helps patients with chronic conditions such as asthma, arthritis, or diabetes to receive timely medications at their doorstep.

Key Features:

  • Doctor consultations
  • Online prescription upload
  • Medicine home delivery from nearby stores
  • Doctor reviews and ratings
  • Revenue generation through delivery services
